description About the Game

Zenless Zone Zero is a brand-new urban fantasy action RPG from HoYoverse. Set in a post-apocalyptic world where mysterious disasters known as 'Hollows' have wiped out most of civilization, New Eridu stands as the last bastion of humanity. Players take on the role of a 'Proxy', a specialized guide who helps others navigate the dangerous Hollows, uncovering secrets and battling ethereal monsters in high-octane, stylish combat.
